I am a Ph.D. student in the Data Mining Lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. U Kang. My research interests cover machine learning under label uncertainty and representation learning for graphs — including PU learning, link (sign) prediction, graph classification, and domain adaptation across graph, tensor, and speech data.
